Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our inventory audit services.

A third-party stock audit is an independent verification of your physical inventory conducted by an external specialist. Unlike internal audits, our auditors have no vested interests in your operations, ensuring unbiased and accurate reporting. This helps uncover discrepancies, prevent pilferage, and provide honest stock data you can rely on.
The duration depends on the size and complexity of your inventory. A typical retail showroom audit takes 1–2 days, while large warehouse audits may take 3–5 days. We aim for 48-hour turnaround on final reports after physical count completion.
